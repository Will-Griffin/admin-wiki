## How to Request Training

1. Identify the course(s) you want to take from this page or your team's training page.
2. Discuss with your manager during your 1:1 or sprint planning.
3. Submit a training request via the appropriate HR or IT procurement process.
4. Complete the course and add your certification/completion to your HR profile.
