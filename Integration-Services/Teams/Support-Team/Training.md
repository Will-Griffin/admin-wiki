## Overview

This page lists recommended training resources for Support Team members. Training is organized by skill category. New team members should prioritize the **Onboarding Track** within their first 90 days; existing members should complete at least one course per quarter.

---

## Recommended Learning Paths

### For New Support Analysts
1. ITIL 4 Foundation (Udemy)
2. Azure Fundamentals AZ-900 (Udemy)
3. KQL from Scratch (Pluralsight)
4. EDI 101 (Udemy)
5. Postman Complete Guide (Udemy)

### For Senior / Lead Support Staff
1. Azure Administration AZ-104 (Udemy)
2. Azure Application Insights (Pluralsight)
3. Azure Data Explorer & KQL (Pluralsight)
4. ITSM Best Practices (Pluralsight)
5. HL7 FHIR Fundamentals (Udemy)
