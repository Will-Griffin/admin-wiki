#Product Owner
The product owner is responsible for maximizing the value of the product resulting from the work of the development team.
The product owner is **one person, not a committee**. A product owner is responsible for prioritizing the backlog. He/she might represent the desires of a committee in the product backlog, but those wanting to change a product backlog item’s priority must be approved by the product owner.
For the product owner to succeed, the entire organization must respect their decisions. These decisions are visible in the content and ordering of the product backlog. No one can force the Dev team to work from a different set of requirements.

#Project Manager
Project managers have the responsibility of the planning, procurement, and execution of a project, in any undertaking that has a defined scope, defined start, and a defined finish, regardless of industry.
Responsibilities that are common to all project managers include:
- Developing project plans
- Managing project stakeholders
- Managing communication
- Managing project teams
- Managing project risks
- Managing project schedule
- Managing project budget
- Managing project conflicts
- Managing project delivery
- Contract administration
