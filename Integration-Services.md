# Integration Services

## Welcome

Welcome to **Integration Services**, part of the **Application Development and Integration Services** Division within the **Chief Information Office Bureau** (CIOB) at the **Department of Mental Health** (DMH). Our team is responsible for DMH's interoperability, API modernization, and integration infrastructure that enables data to move reliably across internal systems, vendor EHRs, State and County departments.

This wiki is written for the team — new and existing members alike — and is meant to be updated continuously as we grow and evolve our practices.

---
