#Integration Services
## Hello

Welcome to **Integration Services** part of the **Application Development and Integration Services** Division within the **Chief Information Office Bureau** (CIOB) at the **Department of Mental Health**. Our team is responsible for DMH’s interoperability, API modernization, and integration infrastructure that enables data to move reliably across internal systems, vendor EHRs, State and County departments.

This guide is written for the team and is meant to be updated continuously as we grow and evolve our practices.
